Software Consultant - Development - Entitlements
Company: ASB
Location: Auckland, New Zealand
Level: 4 (Senior Individual Contributor)

Mō te tūranga | About the role

Join our Core Tribe as a Software Consultant and help shape the Entitlements layer by building the Entitlements engine needed to migrate legacy functions and enable modernisation that powers ASB's digital experiences. This is a senior individual contributor role (Level 4) that blends deep technical expertise with solution architecture, platform ownership, and cross-squad collaboration.

You'll play a key role in enabling seamless, scalable, and secure integration by building reusable components and supporting modern engineering practices across squads.

Ngā kawenga | What you'll do

We advocate for T-Shaped teams, where you will engage in all facets of engineering throughout your day. We are seeking developers with a diverse mix of technical skills to support the following activities:

- Guide implementation using shared SDKs, infrastructure standards, and CI/CD practices, ensuring alignment with security and compliance requirements.
- Work closely with API, integration, and streaming platform teams to design resilient service interactions and meet agreed service levels.
- Evaluate and de-risk emerging technologies, run pilots with clear success criteria and guide adoption across squads.
- Lead delivery of reusable integration components and reference architectures, provide hands-on guidance for complex implementations.
- Engage stakeholders (product, security, ops) to balance velocity with safety, communicate trade-offs and decisions clearly.
