Position Overview:

The Senior Software Engineer plays a key role in the design, development, and delivery of software solutions within the Navico Group PD&E division. This is a hands-on engineering role focused on building reliable, high-quality products while collaborating closely with cross-functional teams.

You will contribute to software architecture, implementation, code quality, and technical problem-solving across a range of products. The role requires strong technical capability, practical engineering judgement, and a collaborative mindset. You will also support and mentor other engineers, helping to drive engineering best practices across the team.

Given evolving product and team requirements, exposure to firmware and embedded systems development will be highly valued.

Essential Functions:

Design, develop, test, and maintain high-quality software systems and applications.
Contribute to technical design discussions and code reviews.
Collaborate with product managers, engineers, and stakeholders to deliver robust solutions.
Support software quality through testing, automation, debugging, and best engineering practices.
Troubleshoot complex software and system-level issues.
Mentor junior engineers and contribute to a strong engineering culture.
Contribute to architecture and technical decisions where appropriate.
Collaborate on software, firmware, and hardware integration as product needs evolve.
