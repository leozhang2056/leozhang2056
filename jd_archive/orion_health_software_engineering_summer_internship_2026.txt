Orion Health
Software Engineering Summer Internships 2026/27

Auckland, Auckland, New Zealand · Hybrid · Contract

About the job
Make your mark on the future of digital health.

At Orion Health, our Software Engineering Summer Internship Programme gives you the opportunity to work on real products, solve meaningful technical challenges, and learn alongside experienced engineers who are building technology that improves healthcare for millions of people around the world.

Over 10-12 weeks, you'll gain hands-on software engineering experience, build practical skills, and see what it's like to work in a global health technology company. You'll be part of a supportive team where your ideas are valued and your work makes a genuine impact.

Why join us?

We believe internships should be meaningful. From your first week, you'll join a real dev team, be contributing to real projects, while receiving the support and mentoring you need to learn and grow.

As an Orion Health Software Engineer Intern, you'll enjoy:

A paid 10-12 week summer internship
A dedicated mentor and support from experienced engineers
Meaningful work on real software projects
Opportunities to learn modern engineering practices, tools and technologies
Exposure to agile software development in a collaborative environment
Networking opportunities with teams across the business
Free breakfast every day and barista-quality coffee
Great office facilities including café spaces, arcade games, pool table, table tennis, PlayStation and pickleball court (Christchurch)
A fun, social workplace with regular team events throughout the summer
The opportunity to be considered for future Graduate Programme opportunities

What you'll be doing

You'll work as part of an engineering team, contributing to products that help healthcare organisations deliver better patient care.

Depending on your team, you could be involved in:

Designing, building and testing software features
Writing clean, maintainable code
Debugging and solving technical problems
Developing APIs and backend services
Working with cloud technologies and modern development tools
Collaborating with product managers, designers and engineers
Participating in agile ceremonies including stand-ups, sprint planning and retrospectives
Throughout the internship you'll receive regular feedback, coaching and opportunities to expand your technical skills while gaining valuable commercial software development experience.

Why Orion Health?

Orion Health is a global healthcare technology company creating software that connects health information and supports better care for millions of people worldwide.

When you join us, you'll work with talented people who love solving complex problems, learn from experienced engineers, and gain experience that's difficult to find elsewhere.
