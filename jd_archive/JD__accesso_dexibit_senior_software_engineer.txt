Description

We're Dexibit. We love dinosaurs and data. Our Kiwi AI product helps visitor attractions (museums to zoos to theme parks) make sense of their data and make better decisions. We work with big international names alongside local favourites like MOTAT and Weta.

Dexibit recently joined accesso, a global technology company serving over 1,000 venues across 30+ countries.

What We Build

Dexibit brings together our deep domain knowledge, AI-ready data platform, served through an agentic interface. We create value for our customers by addressing non-trivial tasks like strategic modelling, scheduling, operational reporting and analysing visitor feedback.

Under the hood, the AI layer orchestrates real business logic and APIs: integration packs, custom datasets and benchmarks, context control features, and our query and visualisation infrastructure.

The agentic interface is where we're investing new feature development. Think streaming, context window management, tool orchestration, sandboxed execution.

You're full-stack, but you lean heavily to one side, whether that's backend data systems or frontend product engineering.
