Work with modern Java and Spring Boot in a growing integration environment
Strong focus on learning, mentorship, and long-term career development
Auckland location, 4 days in the office
About the Company:
This organisation is a well-established, technology-led business undergoing significant digital transformation. With a strong engineering culture, they invest in modern platforms, cloud-native solutions, and people who want to grow their careers.
Why this role matters:
This role supports the development and integration of scalable backend services that enable critical digital platforms and customer-facing systems. As an intermediate engineer, you’ll play an important hands-on role while continuing to build depth in modern backend and integration technologies.
What you’ll lead & influence:
Design, develop and maintain backend services using Java and Spring Boot
Build and support RESTful APIs used across internal and external platforms
Contribute to integration solutions connecting multiple systems and services
Support CI/CD pipelines and deployment processes alongside senior engineers
Collaborate with product, QA, DevOps and architecture teams in an Agile setup
What you bring:
Commercial experience with Java (3-5 years) and Spring Boot
Experience building or supporting REST APIs and backend services
Working knowledge of relational databases (e.g. SQL Server, MySQL or similar)
Exposure to CI/CD tools, containerisation, or cloud platforms is a bonus
Familiarity with tools such as Docker, API gateways, or Azure is nice to have
Most importantly: a positive attitude, curiosity, and a strong desire to learn and grow
Why you’ll love working here:
Genuine
career progression
from intermediate to senior level
Supportive team culture with mentoring from experienced engineers
Exposure to modern integration, API, and cloud technologies
Opportunity to work on meaningful systems, not just isolated components
A workplace that values learning, collaboration, and continuous improvement
