Job Description

An intern Software Engineer is an important software development role without our early careers program. Generally, you will be working alongside Software Engineers with other levels of experience, as well as other scrum team members and consultants.

It is expected that you will be involved in a project that can be completed within the timeframe that you're with us.

There will be a final presentation on this project and the general intern experience.

This role requires a dedication to technical excellence, not only in writing code, but also in team-collaboration, testing, documentation. Good communication skills are essential, as well as a "can-do" attitude and willingness to work across various disciplines, if required.

Software Development
- Developing .NET, SQL, and related front-end code.
- Peer reviews.
- Following coding style standards and guidelines.
- Using source control.
- Using Test Driven Development techniques.
- Writing unit and integration tests.

Domain Knowledge
- Learning Visa Spend Clarity domain concepts.
- Writing documentation.
- Knowledge sharing with other team members.

Agile Mind-set
- Practice agile scrum methodology.
- Contribute to improving team Agile processes.
- Active learning and an open mind.

Visa requires at least 3 days in office, expectations of these days will be confirmed by your Hiring Manager.
