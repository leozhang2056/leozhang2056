Description

Auckland University of Technology (AUT) is bold and unapologetically different, centred on students and grounded in Knowledge that Works driven by talented, progressive people. We are New Zealand's newest university, ranked 5-stars by QS Universities 2025 and a place of opportunity where more than 26,000 students with talent and potential succeed, supported by a community of more than 4,000 staff based primarily across three Auckland campuses. Built on collaboration, shared purpose and the values of Pono (fact, truth, reality, integrity), Tika (acting accordingly, doing what is needed, ethical expression), and Aroha (empathy, care, compassion). AUT offers an innovative, forward-thinking environment where committed people work together to improve outcomes for students, communities, and the world.

AUT is committed to shaping a future where artificial intelligence is used ethically, inclusively and to real effect. We are seeking an AI Capability & Change Lead to play a critical, university-wide role in enabling our people to use AI confidently and effectively in their work.

The Opportunity | Te Whiwhinga mahi

As a Senior Developer, you will play a key role in designing, building, and enhancing AUT's AI and digital experience platforms. You will work across a range of initiatives spanning application development, integrations, cloud services, and AI-enabled solutions, with a strong focus on delivering scalable, user-centred outcomes.

About you | Ko wai koe

This role is ideal for someone who combines solid technical depth with a pragmatic, delivery-focused mindset. You will be confident working across the full development lifecycle, from architecture and prototyping through to production delivery, support, and continuous improvement. You will also provide technical leadership within the team, helping guide good development practices, mentoring less experienced developers, and contributing to a high-performing engineering environment.

To Be Successful In This Role, You Will

- A relevant tertiary qualification in Software Engineering, Computer Science, Information Technology, or a related discipline
- At least 7 years' professional experience in software development
- Strong experience designing, building, and maintaining modern software solutions in a professional environment
- Experience developing APIs and system integrations for reliable data exchange between platforms
- Strong working knowledge of contemporary development approaches including version control, testing, peer review, agile delivery, and CI/CD
- Experience with cloud platforms such as Microsoft Azure, AWS, or similar
- Experience with modern application stacks and development frameworks across frontend, backend, and integration layers
- Strong understanding of software architecture, scalability, maintainability, and supportability
- Awareness of cybersecurity good practice and common application security risks
- Interest in AI, automation, and emerging developer tooling, including AI-assisted development and rapid prototyping
- A commitment to equity, diversity and inclusion. Supportive of AUT's Te Aronui journey
