About the Role

Your ability to navigate ambiguity, communicate clearly, and deliver client-focused solutions will be key to your success. This is a dynamic role suited to someone who thrives in Agile environments, enjoys solving complex problems, and is confident working independently and collaboratively.

This role sits within our Information Services practice - one of four key areas at Tenzing, alongside Technology Services, Management Consulting, and Managed Services. The Information Services team partners with clients to deliver all things data including data science, data warehousing, and data migrations. We use proven methodologies along with industry leading technology (like Snowflake!) to create scalable and sustainable data solutions tailored to our customer's needs.
