Senior Android Developer
MetService · Auckland, New Zealand

This is a unique opportunity to help shape the future of Android development at MetService. As we bring more mobile capability in-house, we are looking for a Senior Android Developer who can play a key role in the design, development and ongoing improvement of our Android apps. These apps are used by New Zealanders every day to access trusted weather information.

You will work closely with backend engineers, designers, product managers and testers to deliver high-quality, reliable and performant mobile experiences. Beyond hands-on development, this role offers the opportunity to bring your technical vision, influence engineering standards, and help define the best way for MetService to build and operate Android products going forward.

In This Role You Will:
- Design, build and enhance secure, high-quality Android features using Kotlin and modern Android frameworks.
- Help shape our Android technical direction, tooling and development standards.
- Work closely with backend, design, product and QA teams to deliver resilient and user-focused mobile experiences.
- Provide senior technical input, guidance and support as our mobile capability grows.

About You:
- At least 5 years Android development experience, particularly with Kotlin and modern Android architecture patterns.
- Key to this role is solving technical problems, improving existing products and making pragmatic engineering decisions.
- Strong collaboration and communication skills.
- Confident sharing ideas, influencing technical direction and helping shape good engineering practices.
- Care about quality, accessibility and performance.
