About the role

Join a high-performing Application Support Team as a Junior Software Configuration Developer. Work on world-class registry solutions for governments and organisations globally. Collaborative, fast-paced environment where contributions directly support national and international registries.

Key Responsibilities:
- Customize software components based on client requirements
- Follow design and specification documents to ensure consistency and quality
- Conduct unit and integration testing of configured components
- Support QA teams during system and user acceptance testing phases
- Provide ongoing production support for live systems
- Identify opportunities for improvement and contribute to enhancing application performance and reliability

What We're Looking For:
- Tertiary qualification in Computer Science, Engineering or related field
- Minimum 2 years experience in an IT environment
- At least 1 year hands-on experience in programming languages (Java preferred, also C#, Groovy, PHP)
- Good understanding of system design, programming concepts, and database fundamentals
- Interest in web technologies (XML, HTML, JavaScript, JSON, CSS)
- Understanding of REST or SOAP web services
- Familiar with SDLC; Agile (Scrum or Kanban) experience a plus
- Practical experience with SDLC tools (Jira, Jenkins)
- Working knowledge of SQL and relational databases
- Familiar with Git, SVN
- Knowledge of ITIL and DevOps practices advantageous
- Exposure to AI tools advantageous
- Good communication and interpersonal skills
- Strong analytical and problem-solving abilities
