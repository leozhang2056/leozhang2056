About Sandfield

We're a Kiwi-owned company that's been around since 1989. Our work facilitates high-performance supply chains for Mainfreight, Qube, HW Richardson, and Booths, and keeps millions of orders and data points flowing for Foodstuffs. It also manages business finances for Team Global Express and Green Acres, helps fill seats on the Interislander for KiwiRail, looks after security services for First Security and books parking spaces through ParkMate, plus so much more.

We pride ourselves on being different from your typical tech company. At Sandfield, you will:
- Solve Real Business Problems: We don't just write code; we seek to understand the "why." A key part of your role will be a deep understanding of business requirements to ensure our solutions deliver maximum impact.
- Leverage AI for Innovation: We are evolving how we work by integrating AI tools to accelerate development and create more intelligent business solutions for our clients.
- Interact Directly with Clients: Our developers deal directly with our clients. We spend just as much time nurturing soft skills, like communication and business acumen, as we do helping you become a coding "ninja."
- Join a Supportive Team: Our collaborative culture is at the centre of everything we do at Sandfield. Average length of stay is more than five years.

Our tech stack

We primarily use the Microsoft stack to build web-based applications, with some opportunities for mobile development. Full-stack experience is ideal, but not required. We'll support you in developing your skills.

Requirements:
