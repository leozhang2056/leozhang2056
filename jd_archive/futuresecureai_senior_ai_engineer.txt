The Opportunity
We're hiring Senior Software Engineers (AI) to help us build our products and platform. You'll work closely with engineering and customer delivery teams, moving between hands-on engineering and broader product and business conversations. If you do your best work when the problem is ambiguous, you'll fit here.

What you'll be doing:
- Building AI Co-Worker systems that enable enterprise customers to deploy persona-based AI Co-Workers that perform complex, end-to-end operational workflows through their own secure platform.
- Ensuring our platform remains model-agnostic, integrating LLMs and AI frameworks from both established and emerging vendors.
- Building and integrating secure, scalable APIs across large-scale enterprise environments.
- Working closely with customers to deeply understand their needs and make sure solutions exceed their expectations.

Your application should demonstrate:
- 7+ years of software development experience with recent hands-on AI work.
- Strong coding fundamentals; we primarily use Node.js and TypeScript.
- Experience with AI frameworks and orchestration tools such as LangChain or LangGraph.
- Experience building systems where security and data governance are first-class design constraints, not afterthoughts.
- Sharp problem-solving instincts and the follow-through to match.
