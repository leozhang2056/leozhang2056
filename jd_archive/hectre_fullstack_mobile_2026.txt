Senior Software Fullstack Engineer - Mobile
Hectre · Auckland, New Zealand (Hybrid)

About the company:
Hectre is the global leader in Fruit Quality AI, on a mission to support the long-term viability of food production for people and the planet. We bring AI to fruit quality and farm management across the supply chain. Since 2016, serving largest fruit growers and packers across 22+ countries, scanning over 3.8 billion pieces of fruit. Products span orchard management, harvest tracking, timesheets, payroll, analytics, and fruit sizing and color grade AI solutions. Multi-award-winning company backed by deep-tech VC.
