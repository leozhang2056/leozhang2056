Gentrack - Intermediate Software Engineer

Role overview

An Intermediate Software Engineer at Gentrack works on developing, maintaining, and supporting software platforms used by energy and water utility companies. The role combines software development, customer-focused problem solving, agile teamwork, and cloud technologies.

Typical responsibilities

Develop, test, deploy, and maintain software applications.

Analyse customer requirements and design technical solutions.

Participate in Agile ceremonies (stand-ups, sprint planning, retrospectives).

Work with technologies such as Java, .NET, AWS, Git, enterprise databases, APIs, CRM, and billing systems.

Perform code reviews and contribute to software quality improvements.

Support customers and provide technical guidance.

Create technical documentation and knowledge-sharing materials.

Collaborate with product managers, architects, testers, and other engineers.

Requirements

Around 3+ years of commercial software development experience.

Experience with Agile development practices.

Knowledge of cloud platforms (especially AWS), object-oriented programming, databases, Git, and web services.

Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Software Engineering, or equivalent experience.

Strong communication and problem-solving skills.
