About the job:
At Halter, we’re on a mission to enable farmers and graziers to run the most productive and sustainable operations. Our customers are using Halter to break free from the time-intensive constraints of conventional practices (GPS collars for cattle fenceless herding).
Halter is one system: hardware, software, and AI, running live on real farms.
We're looking for interns across our Software, Platform, Machine Learning and Comms/Fleet Engineering teams!

We have 4 key teams we're hiring for:
1. Software Engineering:
- Building the farmer-facing apps (mobile/web) and the backend services processing billions of real-time collar events.
- Frontend: React, React Native, mobile and web experiences.
- Backend: Node.js, Kotlin, Go.
- Distributed, event-driven systems processing data from collars; latency, reliability, and offline behaviour are important.

2. Platform Engineering:
- Internal infrastructure, data ingestion, AWS, Terraform, CI/CD, observability, and AI tooling.
- Tooling that helps engineers move faster.

3. Machine Learning (Predictive Systems):
- Pasture growth models, animal health & behaviour, heat event detection, grazing recommendations.
- Dataset construction, model training, deployment, and monitoring. Python, PyTorch.

4. Communications & Fleet Engineering:
- Collars communication with backend, messaging layer, on-farm comms, fleet tracking.
- UART, serial, telemetry, NDK/C.

Who we are looking for:
- Actively engaged in relevant studies (Computer Science, Software Engineering, Data Science).
- Deeply curious, driven to learn, comfortable in fast-moving, ambiguous environments.
- Software/Platform: think like a product engineer, care about customer experience.
- Machine Learning: strong background in applying ML, Python fluency.
- Comfortable leveraging AI tools and agents (Claude Code, Cursor, Copilot) to accelerate output.
