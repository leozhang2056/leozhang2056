Datacenter IT Technician Trainee

Amazon Web Services
Auckland CBD, Auckland
Full time, 6-month trainee contract

About the role
Work with the world's largest computing infrastructure. Learn daily operations of a large data center — hands-on with servers and network equipment. Interact with logistics, networking, and security teams.

Key job responsibilities
- Performing diagnostics and troubleshoot all hardware and related problems
- Performing basic level OS troubleshooting
- Performing basic level configuration troubleshooting
- Perform hardware replacement to support repair actions
- Perform storage media (HDD, SSD etc.) sanitization and destruction tasks
- Maintain a high level of system reliability by prioritizing resolution of trouble tickets efficiently
- Follow DC operations, Logistics, Security and Safety related processes and guidelines
- Practice and enforce Amazon's Security and Safety best practices

Basic Qualifications
- Min. one year of computer hardware or computer network troubleshooting and repair experience
- Physical component: ability to lift and rack equipment using lifting equipment
- Valid driver's license and own vehicle to travel between sites
