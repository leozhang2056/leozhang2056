  Purpose  
As a Senior Android Developer, you will play a key role in the design, development and ongoing 
improvement of MetService’s  Android applications. You will work closely with product managers, 
designers, engineers and delivery leads to build high -quality, performant and resilient mobile 
experiences used by New Zealanders every day. 
This role combines hands -on development with technical leadership, helping set standards, mentor 
others and influence how we build and operate mobile products.

  Person Specification 
Overview 
The successful candidate will have deep experience building native Android applications and a strong 
understanding of modern mobile engineering practices. They will be confident working with Kotlin, 
contemporary Android frameworks, API integrations, app ar chitecture patterns and performance 
optimisation to deliver reliable, scalable mobile experiences. 
Collaboration is central to this role, and the successful candidate will demonstrate the ability to work 
effectively across product, design, QA and backend teams, contributing through technical leadership, 
mentoring and shared problem-solving. 
Knowledge, Skills & Qualifications: 
Required 
• 8+ years of professional experience developing native Android applications  
• Strong proficiency in Kotlin and modern Android frameworks (Jetpack Compose and/or XML 
Views) 
• Strong experience designing and implementing maintainable Android architectures using 
modern Android architecture patterns. 
• Strong understanding of performance, memory management and debugging . 
• Experience with location-based services, mapping or data-heavy applications. 
• Experience with push notification platforms such as Firebase Cloud Messaging).  
• Understanding of accessibility standards and Android accessibility tooling . 
• Ability to own features end-to-end from concept to release. 
• Strong experience building high-performance native mobile features for Android. 
• Experience working closely with backend teams to define and consume RESTful APIs for mobile 
applications. 
• Experience with Android app release processes, signing, Play Console and staged rollouts . 
• Strong experience with Git and mobile CI/CD pipelines using tools such as GitHub Actions , 
Bitrise or Fastlane 
• Understanding of testing practices including unit, integration and end-to-end testing 
• Familiarity with analytics, crash reporting and app observability tooling would be advantageous
