We are looking for an experienced Android Developer to join the OrbitRemit team in Wellington, NZ. In this role, you will take full ownership of our native Android app, shaping the experience for our customers while continuing to grow your skills in a fintech environment.

The Role

Develop with Modern UI: You will spearhead development on our native Android app, which is built on a modern MVVM architecture and written entirely in Kotlin. Your primary focus will be crafting declarative, reactive interfaces using Jetpack Compose.

Bridge the Gap: While we prioritise Compose, you must be skilled in Interoperability. You'll need the expertise to integrate Compose into existing legacy view-based layouts and vice versa, ensuring a seamless experience as we continue our migration.

Own the platform: you must have the confidence to own the codebase. You are the final line of defense for resolving complex issues, managing releases, and making technical decisions that ensure the app remains stable, scalable and projects are delivered on time.

AI-First Mindset: Comfortable leveraging AI-assisted development tools (Claude Code) to accelerate delivery, improve code quality, and explore solutions faster — while applying sound engineering judgement to evaluate and own AI-generated output.

Collaboration: Although you will be the only sole Android developer, you will still work with other developers and product managers to help define and implement new features, you are happy to pair-program if the need arises. And you are generally a team player.

Deep-Dive Debugging: You'll need a sharp eye for the unique quirks of the Android ecosystem. We need a developer who is proficient with Android Studio's profiling tools, capable of hunting down memory leaks, and adept at debugging UI performance issues across a fragmented device landscape.

Architectural Evolution: As the sole Android expert, you will have the autonomy and confidence to modernise the underlying tech stack. You are expected to proactively identify technical debt and refactor core components to improve performance and scalability.

Zero-Downtime Mentality: You must be adept at implementing these deep-level improvements—whether it's migrating legacy View logic to Compose or optimizing the networking layer—without negatively affecting total uptime or app availability. We need a developer who balances bold technical progress with a cautious, "stability-first" approach for our users.

Technical Advocacy: Act as the voice of Android within the business. You'll stay at the forefront of the latest tools and platform updates, strategically incorporating them into our roadmap to keep the app at the cutting edge.
