About the Company:
Our client is a growing digital consultancy partnering with medium to large organisations to deliver digital transformation, intelligent process improvement, compliance solutions and bespoke technology products.
They are passionate about AI, actively embedding AI into their clients’ systems, products and workflows to drive smarter, more efficient outcomes. Their team embraces innovation, continuously exploring and applying AI-driven capabilities to deliver real-world value.
Bringing together technologists, strategists and engineers, they combine deep expertise with a collaborative mindset to deliver measurable impact and modern, scalable solutions.
Why this role matters:
As an Intermediate Developer, you’ll contribute to building high‑quality, modern applications across a diverse technology stack, with a particular focus on
Java and TypeScript
.
Your work will support digital platforms, automation initiatives and AI-enabled solutions that enable smarter, more efficient business operations. This is an opportunity to broaden your technical experience while making a tangible impact across multiple client engagements.
We’re ideally looking for someone who can
start immediately
and quickly contribute across delivery projects. You'll work closely with one particular client to start and can move onto other client projects. You'll spend time at the clients office, the consultancy's office & they offer hybrid working.
What you’ll lead & influence:
Design, develop and maintain applications with a strong focus on Java and TypeScript (NodeJS and modern front-end frameworks)
Contribute to solution design, technical discussions and building scalable, pragmatic approaches
Work across AI-enabled and data-driven solutions, helping embed intelligent capabilities into client platforms
Collaborate with Business Analysts, Designers and senior engineers to deliver high-quality outcomes
Write clean, maintainable and well-tested code following best practices and modern engineering standards
Support continuous improvement through refactoring, optimisation and system enhancements
Participate in Agile ceremonies and contribute to a strong team delivery culture
What you bring:
3–5 years’ commercial software development experience
Strong experience in
Java and/or TypeScript
(highly preferred)
Experience with modern frameworks (NodeJS, front-end JS frameworks, or backend services)
Solid understanding of APIs, integration patterns and modern application architecture
Experience working in Agile environments
Collaborative mindset with strong problem-solving capability
Bonus: Exposure to
AI/ML initiatives
, AWS or Azure, CI/CD pipelines or automation
Why you’ll love working here:
You’ll join a forward-thinking consultancy that lives and breathes AI and innovation, where curiosity, ownership and continuous learning are highly valued.
Expect exposure to a wide range of technologies, the chance to work across multiple industries and the opportunity to contribute to cutting-edge AI-driven solutions that make a meaningful, real-world impact.
