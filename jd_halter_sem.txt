About Halter
Halter is on a mission to enable farmers and graziers to run the most productive and sustainable operations. Our customers are using Halter to break free from the time-intensive constraints of conventional practices. People join Halter to do meaningful work. By joining us you'll be solving challenging problems within a talented team and a culture built for high performance. Our team out-think, out-work and out-care.

About the role
Imagine taking one of the world's most advanced ag-tech platforms and levelling it up with AI-powered products built to last — not demos, not prototypes, but real software that ships, scales, and makes a genuine difference.

This role is for a Senior Software Engineer who happens to love AI, someone who cares deeply about system design, clean architecture, and building full-stack products end-to-end. You'll be working across the stack to design, build, and scale the AI-powered tools and surfaces that our customers, support teams, and commercial teams rely on every day.

You'll sit at the heart of a rapidly growing AI ecosystem at Halter, collaborating closely with our Customer AI, Support, Commercial, and launch teams. From polished frontend experiences to robust backend services and APIs, you'll own meaningful slices of our product and infrastructure.

In this role your focus will be internal tooling — think analytics tools, commission tooling etc.

What we're looking for:
- Key tech/experience: React, React Native, AI, Mobile Development, Full stack with Front End focus
- Strong full-stack engineering skills across frontend and backend — ideally TypeScript/React on the front, Python and/or Node on the back
- Solid experience designing and building production-grade APIs, services, and data pipelines
- A good intuition for how to integrate AI capabilities (LLMs, RAG, agents) into real products without the tech becoming the product
- Experience building for scale: writing maintainable code, thinking about observability, reliability, and performance from day one
- Comfort working across the stack — you're not precious about where a problem lives, you just want to solve it well
- A collaborative approach — you enjoy working tightly with cross-functional teams and bringing others along with you

Bonus points:
- Experience with AI frameworks like LangChain, LlamaIndex, or similar
- Familiarity with vector databases, embeddings, or retrieval systems
- Exposure to workflow orchestration tooling (n8n, Temporal, Airflow, etc.)
- Experience with data engineering, ETL pipelines, or event-driven architectures
- Comfort working in fast-paced, ambiguous environments — you actually enjoy the uncertainty

What you'll be doing:
- Build and ship full-stack AI-powered features across Halter's commercial, support, and internal platforms
- Design and implement backend services and APIs that power AI workflows at scale
- Build clean, intuitive frontend experiences
- Own end-to-end delivery of features: scoping, architecting, building, testing, deploying, and iterating
- Work with AI models and pipelines as a tool in your engineering toolkit — integrating them thoughtfully into well-designed systems
- Contribute to Halter's engineering standards around reliability, observability, testing, and maintainability
- Collaborate with the Customer AI team to help define how AI capabilities get built and delivered across the product
