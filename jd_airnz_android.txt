Senior Mobile Engineer (Android)
Air New Zealand
185 Fanshawe Street, Auckland, New Zealand
Full-time

About the Role
As a Senior Mobile Engineer, you will play a pivotal role in bringing Air New Zealand's digital ambition to life. Your work will directly power the mobile experiences that help customers search, book, manage, and enjoy their journeys, delivering seamless, reliable interactions at scale.

Air New Zealand is on an accelerated journey to become the world's leading digital airline. Within our Digital team, you will join a dedicated product squad, collaborating closely with Product, Design, and Engineering peers to create intuitive, high performing Android applications.

Responsibilities:
- Design and develop advanced Android applications that support millions of customer interactions each year
- Contribute to scalable, secure, and high-performance architecture that enables rapid product evolution
- Collaborate with Product Managers, UI and UX Designers, and backend engineers to translate ideas into elegant mobile solutions
- Improve app performance, reliability, and maintainability through continuous optimisation and technical excellence
- Identify, troubleshoot, and resolve complex bugs and performance issues to ensure a seamless customer experience
- Champion modern Android practices and help uplift engineering standards across the squad
- Demonstrated experience mentoring engineers and providing technical leadership

Requirements:
- At least 5 years of Android development experience, ideally within large scale or complex environments
- Deep expertise in Kotlin and Jetpack Compose, with working knowledge of Java
- Strong understanding of Android SDK, RESTful APIs, and modern mobile architecture patterns
- Experience with Swift, backend for frontend patterns, deployments, or AWS, advantageous but not essential
- Experience using AI tooling to enhance development workflows
- Demonstrated experience mentoring engineers and providing technical leadership
- A passion for clean, maintainable code and delivering quality at pace
- The ability to thrive in a fast paced, collaborative product environment

Benefits:
- Discounted flights for you and your whānau, plus access to standby travel across a global network
- FlexiLeave, with the option to purchase up to four additional weeks of leave each year
- A strong learning culture with on-the-job development and growth opportunities
