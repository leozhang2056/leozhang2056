About the company

Fully onsite role — no hybrid or remote arrangements.

- Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Information Systems, or Information Technology

- Hands-on experience with at least one modern front-end framework: React, Angular, or Vue

- Able to work fully onsite at North Shore, Auckland office
