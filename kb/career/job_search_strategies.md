# 求职策略

> 来源：AUT Employability & Careers

---

## 核心数据

| 事实 | 含义 |
|---|---|
| NZ 只有 **30%** 岗位公开 | 70% 在隐藏市场 |
| 一个岗位 **50–200+** 申请 | 海投同一份 CV 无效 |
| 雇主偏好**人脉招聘** | 省时、省成本、可信度高 |

---

## 两种策略并行

### 被动求职
- Seek / TradeMe / LinkedIn Jobs
- 公司官网 Careers
- 社交媒体关注公司
- Job Alerts

### 主动求职
- 人脉推荐
- 直接邮件/电话/Walk-in
- LinkedIn 连接业内人士
- 招聘中介
- 志愿者/实习切入

---

## 求职管理

- Excel 记录每次投递（公司、岗位、日期、状态）
- 截图保存 JD
- 启用 Job Alerts
- 投后一周无回复 → follow up

## 直接联系雇主模板

**邮件：**
> Kia ora, I'm [name], a [year] [degree] student at AUT. I'm keen to get involved with [org]. I would love the opportunity to chat about potential opportunities. I'm available for [part-time/roles]. CV attached. Ngā Mihi

**Walk-in：**
> Hi, I'm [name]. I'm currently a full-time student looking for part-time work. I'm studying [subject] and interested in [field]. Would you like a copy of my CV or prefer email? Thank you for your time.
