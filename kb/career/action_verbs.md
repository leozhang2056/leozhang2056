# Action Verbs — CV / CL 动作动词表

> 来源：AUT Employability & Careers

---

## 原则

- 用 JD 用的语言写 CV
- 每个 bullet：「动作 + 成果」
- 不要写 "worked in"，写 "Delivered / Built / Led"

## 示例

| 不要写 | 要写 |
|---|---|
| Worked in customer service | Greeted customers, explained product benefits |
| | Contributed to meeting daily sales targets |
| | Received top salesperson award 4 times in 2 years |
