Who we are:
Vista Group brings more people together to experience the magic of movies and cinema by providing a platform solution that connects the industry and powers the moviegoer experience. We are a New Zealand company with a global footprint, providing an innovative range of complementary solutions across the film industry, from production and distribution to cinema exhibition through to the experience of the moviegoer.

Our People:
With more than 700 people across the globe our people are our most important asset. We are proud of our diversity and have placed a high priority on being an inclusive workplace that is reflective of our clients and the communities we serve. It is important to us to foster an environment that empowers people to realise their full potential. We aim to do 'good things with good people' and we're proud to say that more than two decades on that's exactly what we do every single day.


Requirements

When it comes to Cinema, we've got things covered…

Cinema Websites, Mobile app, loyalty program, Digital signage, POS systems, Kiosks, ticket scanning.

In true movie fashion, there's so much more behind the scenes too! Scheduling films and trailers, vouchers and gift cards, refunds, payments, and working with staff are all elements our software targets, all in the Cloud.

We have an exciting opportunity for a Graduate Software Engineer to join Vista Group, for candidates who have graduated and are available to start immediately.
