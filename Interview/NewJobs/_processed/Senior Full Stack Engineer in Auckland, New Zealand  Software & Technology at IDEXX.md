JOB DESCRIPTION

IDEXX continues to invest heavily in Veterinary Software as we scale our platforms and strengthen our engineering capability to meet future growth and complexity.

Auckland is home to our global Software Centre of Excellence, where we design and build cloud-based platforms used by veterinary practices worldwide. We’re looking for a Senior Full Stack Engineer who is strong in **.NET** and passionate about **modern frontend development** to join the team working on SmartFlow.

**About SmartFlow**

[**SmartFlow**](https://www.idexx.com/en/veterinary/software-services/smartflow/) is our patient workflow solution that helps veterinary teams streamline clinical processes, improve visibility, and eliminate bottlenecks in busy practices. It plays a critical role in day-to-day clinic operations.

This is a hands-on senior engineering role with strong full stack ownership. You’ll contribute across the entire application — from backend services and APIs in.NET, through to modern frontend components — ensuring solutions are scalable, secure, and maintainable including leading a significant migration from AngularJS to React, while contributing across the full stack.

**What you’ll be doing**

- Leading by example — actively contributing high-quality code across.NET and React
- Driving frontend architecture and best practices, particularly through the AngularJS → React transformation
- Designing and delivering scalable, secure, and maintainable solutions in a cloud-based SaaS environment
- Collaborating closely with Product Managers and Practice Leads to deliver fit-for-purpose solutions
- Championing pragmatic technical improvements (refactoring, debt reduction, performance, maintainability)
- Influencing engineering standards, practices, and technical direction across squads
- Supporting a strong engineering culture focused on quality, automation, and continuous improvement

**What you’ll bring**

- Proven experience as a Senior Software Engineer in a SaaS or product-led environment
- Strong backend capability in C# /.NET
- Deep experience with modern frontend development, ideally React
- Experience modernising or migrating legacy frontend frameworks (AngularJS experience beneficial)
- Practical knowledge of software architecture and systems design
- Experience building and operating in cloud-based environments
- A strong advocate for automated testing, DevSecOps, and CI/CD practices
- Experience working within Agile frameworks (Scrum, Kanban, or similar)
- Ability to influence technically and culturally — mentoring others and raising engineering standards

**What you can expect from us:**

- Structured onboarding, induction, ongoing training and transparent performance driven professional development
- Rewarding salary packages including lucrative incentive schemes for all
- Continuous L&D (both internal and externally)
- Great tech kit
- Hybrid working with a great office space to collaborate - including a gym and roof top terrace!
- Coffee + Fruit + Food Truck Friday
- Dog friendly office
- Fully subsidized Southern Cross Health insurance
- Part subsidized parking via Parkable
- Career pathways that will take you places if you are willing and able

If you want work with real purpose with a growing company solving key challenges in the animal health industry with innovative cloud software, this is the opportunity for you! 

**So, what is IDEXX Veterinary Software?**  

Here in Auckland we develop a portfolio of cloud platforms and applications. We’re building a vertical SaaS platform for animal health that requires significant technical expertise to deliver. Solutions for practice management (PIMS), diagnostics, payments, client engagement, analytics & insight plus many more. We’re on a mission to build the best health technology business and we need you to help us do it! You can learn more about IDEXX Veterinary Software here: [https://careers.idexx.com/us/en/veterinary-software](http://careers.idexx.com/us/en/veterinary-software)

At IDEXX we are dedicated to enhancing the health and well-being of pets, people, and livestock around the world. Through the power of the cloud, IDEXX Veterinary Software solutions simplify the flow of information across any practice and link vets to what they need when they need it. Patient data. Client communications. Business insights. All at their fingertips.

Our journey is solving veterinary practices’ most pressing needs by focusing on innovation to drive meaningful improvements in animal health.

**Why IDEXX**

We’re proud of the work we do, because our work matters. An innovation leader in every industry we serve, we follow our Purpose and Guiding Principles to help pet owners worldwide keep their companion animals healthy and happy, to ensure safe drinking water for billions, and to help farmers protect livestock and poultry from disease. We have customers in over 175 countries and a global workforce of over 10,000 talented people.

So, what does that mean for you? We enrich the livelihoods of our employees with a positive and respectful work culture that embraces challenges and encourages learning and discovery. At IDEXX, you will be supported by competitive compensation, incentives, and benefits while enjoying purposeful work that drives improvement.

Let’s pursue what matters together.
