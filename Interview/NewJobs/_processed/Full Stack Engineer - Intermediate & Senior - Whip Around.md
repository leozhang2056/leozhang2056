Full job description
Join a Kiwi SaaS company entering its next growth chapter.

At Whip Around, we're helping fleets work smarter, safer, and more efficiently — with customers across New Zealand, the US, and beyond.

Now things are getting interesting.

Following a recent majority investment from Accel-KKR (AKKR), we're accelerating growth, investing in product innovation, and building out our engineering capability here in Auckland.

That means an opportunity for engineers who want more than just another sprint cycle.

Why this role?
You'll join a product squad building software used every day by real-world operators — solving problems that genuinely matter.


You'll help shape:

New product experiences and platform capability
Smarter developer tooling and engineering practices
How we leverage AI (generative + agentic) to improve both product and developer experience
Scalable systems as we continue global growth
This is the kind of role where you can have real influence, ship meaningful work, and grow alongside a business with momentum.


Requirements

What you'll bring
You don't need to tick every box, but you'll likely have:


Strong commercial experience with modern web applications
React experience on the front end
Backend experience (PHP/Laravel is helpful but not essential)
A quality mindset — clean code, testing, collaboration
Curiosity, pragmatism, and a builder mentality
A genuine interest in solving customer problems

Bonus points if you enjoy experimenting, improving systems, and bringing fresh thinking to engineering.
