## About the job

Wētā FX is a world leading visual effects studio with a strong track record of delivering complex, high-quality work across film and television, working on large scale productions that push both creative and technical boundaries.  
  
We are currently seeking Software Engineers to join the Technology and Research realm here in Wellington. These roles focus on solving technical issues for Wētā FX users while contributing to the development of tools that support our crew to deliver world class CGI. Our Engineers specialise in creating robust, scalable tools that support hundreds of artists while adapting to the creative and technical challenges of each new project.  
  
In these roles, you will be working across large scale, visual effects of feature films and television, integrating new and emerging technologies into production workflows, and collaborating with teams across the T&R and wider artistry realms. This is a great opportunity to grow within a high performing environment and work on complex, evolving problem spaces.  
  
**What You’ll Be Doing**

- Writing scalable, robust code that integrates with an array of Wētā FX custom libraries
- Trouble-shooting software issues and providing support to a wide range of users
- Delivering pragmatic solutions in a fast paced, constantly evolving environment
- Working with stakeholders and teammates to strategize the best solutions
- Coding, testing, integration, and release cycles that keep production safe and moving ahead.
**What We’re Looking For**
- Strong Python development skills or experience with a similar programming language
- Experience developing within DCC tools, such as Houdini or Autodesk Maya
- Familiarity with Git version control systems
- A solid understanding of software engineering principles, including architecture, design patterns and unit/integration/regression/acceptance testing
- Self-motivated, approachable, proactive, adaptable, and a hard-working teammate with a collaborative spirit
- Experienced in providing technical assistance to deliver projects with internal/external clients. Able to thrive in fast-paced/time-critical environments. Able to collect requirements and user feedback independently
- Good communicator, both written and verbal. You’ll work with developers, non-technical people, and leadership to understand feature requests and solve production problems.
**You might also have**
- Experience with other common scripting languages such as C++, C# or Java
- Experience with desktop GUI frameworks such as Qt
- Experience in API development, with a focus on performance, stability and scalability
- Background in Visual effects or video game development
This opportunity extends to NZ/AU residents and citizens living in Wellington, New Zealand.  
  
At Wētā FX, we celebrate diversity and strive to create an environment where all our team members feel welcomed, respected, supported, and appreciated. We define ourselves by the quality of our work and are committed to building an unmatched workplace experience.  
  
We aim to provide a discrimination-free experience, beginning from the moment you apply for a role with us. If there is anything we can do to make the recruitment process easier, please let us know at recruitment@wetafx.co.nz.  
  
Location: Wellington, New Zealand
