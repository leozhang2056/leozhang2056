**Kōrero mō te tūranga - About the role**

As a Senior Android Developer, you will play a key role in the **design, development and ongoing improvement** of MetService’s Android applications. You will work closely with **backend engineers, designers and product managers** to build high-quality, performant and resilient mobile experiences used by New Zealanders every day.

This role combines hands-on development with senior technical contribution, helping shape standards, provide guidance for how we build and operate Android mobile products.

**In this role you will:**

- Design, build and enhance **secure, high-quality** Android features using **Kotlin** and modern Android frameworks.
- Partner with **backend engineers, designers, product managers and testers** to deliver resilient mobile experiences.
- Shape Android engineering standards, support other developers and improve the way we build and operate our apps.

**He kōrero mōu – About you**

- You bring deep experience building native Android apps with Kotlin and modern architecture patterns.
- You have strong collaboration and communication skills and can work effectively across disciplines.
- You care about quality, accessibility and performance, and enjoy contributing to a collaborative engineering culture.

**Ētahi kōrero hai āwhina I a koe - Why you should join our team**

We enjoy achieving, we appreciate **creativity**, and we recognise everyone’s unique skills and qualities. This allows us to be passionate about what we do; and to deliver products and services that have positive outcomes for our commercial customers and the people of New Zealand.
