### Key Services Hnry Provides:

- **Automated Tax Deductions:** Whenever you receive a payment from a client, Hnry automatically calculates and deducts the appropriate tax (such as Income Tax, GST, and ACC levies) and sends them to the tax authorities (IRD) on your behalf. The remaining "net" amount is then deposited into your personal bank account.
    
- **Tax Filing:** They handle your tax filings and ensure you remain compliant with **tax regulations**.
    
- **Expense Management:** You can submit your business expenses through the platform. Hnry helps you manage these to ensure you receive the appropriate tax relief, which lowers your overall tax burden.
    
- **Invoicing & Payments:** It provides tools to easily generate professional invoices and get paid by clients.
    
- **Business Banking:** The platform provides you with a dedicated account to separate your business finances from your personal ones.
    

Hnry is a world-first accounting service, that automatically calculates, pays, and files taxes for **sole traders**. We’re on a mission to make it easy, affordable, and accessible for anyone to earn independently. Whether you’re a freelancer, contractor, or side hustler, we remove the barriers that stand between sole traders and the freedom to build their own career. No more complicated tax time, or stress about managing finances—Hnry handles it all for you so you can never think about tax again.
