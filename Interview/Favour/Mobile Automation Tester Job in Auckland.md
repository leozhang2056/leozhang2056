**Tō kamupene hou | Your new company**  
  
They are a purpose‑driven organisation that plays a meaningful role in communities across New Zealand. Their digital platforms are used by hundreds of thousands of customers, and quality, trust, and reliability are at the heart of everything they build.  
  
**Tō tūranga hou | Your new role**

They are looking for a Senior Test Engineer (Native Mobile Apps) to join a growing engineering team and help deliver reliable, high-quality mobile experiences. This is a hands-on automation role focused on testing native iOS and Android applications-you'll spend your time building, maintaining, and evolving mobile test automation frameworks, rather than writing production app code.  
You'll play a key role in shaping their approach to mobile test automation, working closely with developers and delivery teams to ensure quality is embedded from the very start of the development lifecycle. You will be,  
- Designing, building, and maintaining robust automation frameworks for native iOS and Android apps
- Creating and executing automated test scripts using Appium and WebdriverIO
- Writing clear, behaviour‑driven test scenarios using Cucumber (BDD)
- Integrating automated tests into CI/CD pipelines to provide fast, reliable feedback
- Running tests across a range of devices and environments using BrowserStack App Automate

**Ngā uara me ngā pūkenga e angitu ai koe | What you'll need to succeed**

- A proven track record in native mobile test automation across iOS and Android
- Strong hands‑on experience with Appium and WebdriverIO, building scalable test suites
- Proficiency in TypeScript or JavaScript
- Confidence working with Cucumber and BDD practices
- Experience integrating automated tests into CI/CD pipelines
- Familiarity with BrowserStack App Automate or similar cloud‑based device platforms
