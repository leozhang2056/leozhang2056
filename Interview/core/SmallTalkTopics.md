# Small Talk Common Topics

English small talk quick reference for overseas job search, workplace, and social settings.

## Core Principles

- **Short, quick, light** — avoid complex grammar
- **Ask more open-ended questions** — "How did you get into that?" instead of "Do you like it?"
- **Essence** — Make others feel comfortable talking to you, not information exchange

---

## Quick Reference by Scenario

### First Meeting
| Scenario | Expression |
|---|---|
| Self-introduction | Hi, I'm Leo. Nice to meet you. / I just moved to Auckland recently. |
| Being introduced | Nice to finally meet you. / Good to put a face to the name. |
| Asking about work | What do you do? / What line of work are you in? |
| Answering about work | I work in IT. / I do Android and backend dev. / I'm currently looking for full-time work. |
| Talking about hometown | I'm from China. / I'm based in Auckland now. / I moved here for study. |
| Ending | Great talking with you. / Hope to see you around. |
| Catching up | How've you been? / Long time no see. / What have you been up to? |

### Workplace
| Scenario | Expression |
|---|---|
| Morning | Morning! / Ready for another busy day? |
| Elevator | Busy day today? / Weather's getting colder. |
| Pre-meeting | Crazy week so far. / Hope everyone survived Monday. |
| Lunch invitation | Want to grab lunch later? / Keen to join us? |
| Company event | First time here? / Nice turnout tonight. |

### Networking
| Scenario | Expression |
|---|---|
| Party icebreaker | How do you know the host? / Great party. |
| Talking about food | This is really good. / What are you drinking? |
| LinkedIn exchange | Let's connect on LinkedIn. / What kind of projects are you working on? |
| Business networking | What brings you here? / What industry are you in? |

### Daily Life
| Scenario | Expression |
|---|---|
| Coffee shop | Busy today, huh? / Worth the wait. |
| Uber | Been busy today? / Traffic's not too bad. |
| Gym | Mind if I use this after you? / Great workout. |
| Neighbor | How's your week been? / Beautiful weather today. |
| Supermarket | Busy afternoon. / Looks like rain later. |

### Universal Topics
| Topic | Expression |
|---|---|
| Weather | Beautiful day today. / Auckland weather changes so fast. |
| Weekend | Got any plans this weekend? / Doing anything fun? / Just taking it easy. |
| Travel | Been anywhere nice recently? / NZ has amazing scenery. |
| Movies/TV | Seen anything good lately? / No spoilers please. / That show is addictive. |
| Pets | Your dog is adorable. / What breed is he? |

### Techniques
| Scenario | Expression |
|---|---|
| Ending conversation | I'll let you mingle. / Great chatting with you. / Catch you later. |
| Didn't catch that | Sorry, could you say that again? / I didn't quite catch that. |

## Practice Suggestions

15-20 minutes daily: Learn 3-5 scenarios → Read aloud → Substitute with your own experiences → Review the next day.
