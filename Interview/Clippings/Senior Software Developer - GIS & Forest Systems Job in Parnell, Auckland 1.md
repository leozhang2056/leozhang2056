**The role**  
Your primary focus is the ongoing development and operations of Nateva's Forest and Estate Management System - a .NET/MAUI application running on Azure that integrates with our GIS mapping platform. You'll own the architecture and data-layer integration between Azure SQL and PostGIS, manage external development partners, and help translate business needs into practical technology roadmaps.  
You don't need to be a GIS expert coming in, but you'll quickly develop a good working understanding of our spatial systems, and that knowledge will make you a significantly more effective developer in this environment. You'll also mentor colleagues across the business in building automation capability using AI-assisted Python and R.  
  
**What you'll bring**  
- 5+ years software development experience
- 5+ years database administration (SQL, preferably with some PostGIS exposure)
- Experience in .NET, Python or R
- Familiarity with Azure or similar cloud environments
- Some GIS exposure (QGIS a plus, not essential)
- Strong communication skills - you'll be translating between technical and operational teams
- A full driver's licence and willingness to travel to forests around NZ (overnight stays required)
