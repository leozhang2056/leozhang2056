**About the Company  
**  
This large, well‑established New Zealand organisation operates at national scale and is undergoing significant transformation across technology, digital platforms and customer experience.

**Why this role matters**  
  
This Test Automation Engineer role is critical to ensuring the quality, stability and performance of complex digital platforms and enterprise systems.  
  
It suits someone who enjoys variety, context‑switching and contributing across both project delivery and BAU work within a fast‑paced agile environment.  
  
**What you’ll lead & influence**
- Lead end‑to‑end testing activities across functional, integration, regression, SIT and UAT phases
- Design, build and execute **Playwright automation using C#**, alongside hands‑on manual testing
- Perform API and integration testing across complex systems and workflows
- Collaborate closely with Product Owners, Business Analysts, Developers and stakeholders
- Manage defects through the full lifecycle and contribute to agile delivery ceremonies
- Support continuous improvement of testing practices, including modern automation and AI‑enabled testing approaches
**What you bring**
- 5+ years’ experience in software testing within agile delivery environments within well-known enterprise organisations
- Strong hands‑on experience with **Playwright automation using C#**
- Experience across UI, integration and API testing, with basic SQL capability
- Confidence working across multiple projects and changing priorities
- Familiarity with CI/CD workflows and tools such as Postman, JIRA, qTest or Zephyr
- Experience with Workday or similar financial systems (highly regarded)
