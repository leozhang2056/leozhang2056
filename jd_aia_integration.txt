Your Role with Us
As a Software Engineer Specialist – Integration in our Technology team, you'll play a key role in designing and delivering integration solutions that keep our systems connected, secure, and running smoothly. You'll collaborate with architects, engineers, and business stakeholders to build robust, scalable platforms that support both strategic initiatives and day-to-day operations.

Your Impact
- Design and deliver integration solutions that enable reliable, secure connectivity and support business outcomes across the organisation.
- Develop and enhance integration components that meet quality standards, improving system performance and maintainability.
- Resolve incidents and strengthen reliability, contributing to improved system stability and seamless operations.
- Collaborate with stakeholders to clarify requirements and deliver solutions that align with business needs and expectations.
- Drive continuous improvement by contributing to platform roadmaps and evolving integration practices for long-term success.

Your Capabilities
- Strong experience in integration or C# .NET development, with the ability to deliver solutions in complex environments.
- Relevant tertiary qualification in IT, Computer Science, or a related discipline.
- Communicate clearly and build effective relationships, enabling collaboration with both technical and non-technical stakeholders.
- Apply analytical thinking and sound judgement, balancing quality, risk, and delivery outcomes.
- Take ownership and contribute to team success, managing priorities while supporting others through knowledge sharing.
- Relevant professional certifications are a plus.

Why AIA
